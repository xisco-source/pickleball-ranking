Privacy Policy

Last updated: September 5, 2025

This Privacy Policy explains how the Pickleball Cayman Ranking Assistant ("the App") handles information.

Information We Collect

  • The App only processes the player names that you manually enter.
  • No accounts, passwords, payment details, or location data are collected.
  • The App does not use cookies, tracking pixels, or analytics services.

How We Use Information

  • Player names are used solely to fetch corresponding rankings from public sources.
  • Rankings are displayed back to you in the App for comparison and grouping.
  • No data you enter is stored, logged, or shared.

Data Sources

  • The App retrieves rankings from publicly available data on pickleball.ky.
  • The App may use a CORS-friendly proxy (e.g., r.jina.ai) to fetch ranking pages. This proxy is used only to enable in-browser fetching and does not intentionally store your input.

Third Parties

  • No personal data is shared with third parties.
  • The App does not include advertising or marketing integrations.

Your Privacy

  • Since no personal data is collected or stored, there is nothing to delete or export.
  • If you have concerns about privacy, you may simply stop using the App.

Contact

For questions about this Privacy Policy, please contact the developer at: infosystemsky@gmail.com.
